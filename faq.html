Membership & Access

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, all plans are month-to-month with no long-term contract required.

Is there a joining fee?

No joining fee for Elite and Pro plans. Basic has a one-time ₹500 setup fee.

What are the operating hours?

Pro and Elite members get 24/7 access. Basic members can train 5am–11pm daily.

Can I bring a guest?

Pro members get 2 guest passes per month. Other plans can purchase day passes at the front desk.

Training & Recovery

How often should I train per week?

Most members see best results training 4–5 days a week with at least one full rest day.

Do I need to stretch before lifting?

Dynamic warm-ups before, static stretching after — this reduces injury risk and improves range of motion.

How long until I see results?

Most members notice strength gains within 3–4 weeks and visible changes within 8–12 weeks, consistency depending.

Should I re-rack my weights?

Always. Re-racking weights is standard gym etiquette and keeps the floor safe for everyone.

Myth vs Fact

Myth

Lifting weights makes women bulky.

Fact

Building visible bulk requires specific high-volume training and surplus eating — strength training alone leads to lean, toned muscle.

Myth

You need to train every day to see results.

Fact

Rest days are when muscle actually repairs and grows — 4–5 quality sessions beat 7 exhausted ones.

Myth

Cardio kills muscle gains.

Fact

Moderate cardio supports recovery and heart health without meaningfully affecting muscle growth when protein intake is sufficient.

Myth

You can spot-reduce fat in one area.

Fact

Fat loss happens across the whole body based on genetics and overall calorie deficit — not from targeting one muscle group.
